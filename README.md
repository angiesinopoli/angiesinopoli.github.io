# Angie Sinopoli — Portfolio

My personal portfolio site, built to showcase my projects, experience, and a little bit of what I'm about outside of code. Live at [angiesinopoli.github.io](https://angiesinopoli.github.io).

## What's here

- **About me** — a short intro, plus a definition-box callout for the word that best describes how I approach problems: resourceful.
- **My work** — featured projects, including [LSAT Liaison](https://chatgpt.com/g/g-6a26e23f6c1481918996754485b6d822-lsat-liaison), an AI-powered LSAT tutoring system, along with my Java, full-stack, and coursework projects.
- **Experience & skills** — my background in IT support and software development, and the languages and tools I work in.
- **What I'm listening to** — a handful of albums in rotation.
- **Resume & contact** — links to reach me directly.

## Built with

This site is built on top of [Stellar by HTML5 UP](https://html5up.net/stellar), customized with:

- Custom typography (Playfair Display + Nunito)
- A canvas-based constellation animation in the header background
- Content, styling, and copy tailored to me
