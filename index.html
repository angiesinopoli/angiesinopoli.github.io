About Me

I'm Angie, from Cleveland. I found coding in a high school elective my senior year and it clicked in a way not much else had: the logic, the precision, the way a problem keeps nagging at you until you finally crack it. I came into Ohio State as a CIS student without a clear plan and have been figuring it out ever since.

Most of my work is in Java, with HTML, JavaScript, and C in progress. I care a lot about writing code that is clean and readable. When I'm not in class or at a terminal I'm usually finding a new coffee shop, out hiking, or at home with my dogs Thor and Sparkle. I don't have a rehearsed answer for where I'm headed after graduation. Wherever I end up, I want to have built something worth showing.
