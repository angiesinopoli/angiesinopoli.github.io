about me

resourceful /rəˈsôrsf(ə)l/

being resourceful means having the cleverness and initiative to solve problems and overcome difficulties, often by effectively utilizing the materials or tools at hand. it is a highly valued trait defined by adaptability, creative thinking, and the ability to thrive when faced with new or challenging situations.

hi! i'm Angie, a CIS student at Ohio State from Cleveland. i first got into coding in high school, but it was not until my harder college classes that i realized how much i liked being challenged by it. school had always come pretty easily to me, so having to really work through a problem made finally solving it that much more satisfying.

i'm practical, independent, and resourceful, but i also do not always fit the mold people expect in tech. i'm outgoing, people oriented, and i think my ability to communicate, build relationships, and make people feel comfortable is one of my biggest strengths.

most of my experience is in Java and HTML, and i'm continuing to learn PowerShell, JavaScript, CSS, and C. outside of school, i'm usually at the gym, trying a new coffee shop, spending time with friends, or at home with my dogs, Thor and Sparkle. i'm still figuring out exactly where i want to end up, but i know i want to keep solving meaningful problems, building things i'm proud of, and proving that i belong in the room.

since then, i have built experience across troubleshooting, access management, virtual desktop support, PowerShell automation, process documentation, and collaboration among multiple teams. the variety has taught me how to adapt quickly, communicate clearly, recognize patterns, and work through problems that look completely different from one day to the next.
